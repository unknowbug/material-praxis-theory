> **⚠️ On the Term "Material":** The term *"Material"* in Material-Praxis Theory does **not** denote ontological materialism (i.e., the claim that "the world is made of matter"). Rather, it refers to the **operational anchoring** of all cognitive claims in material practice — the **A-layer (Material Substrate)** of the A-B-C framework. MPT explicitly rejects ontology as a valid cognitive enterprise. "A = material" is an operational definition ("the endpoint of verification is physical causality"), not an ontological assertion. See §1 below.

**Core Positioning**: This framework is a universal **"cognition-practice system dynamics"** meta-analytical tool and operating manual, applicable to any domain involving **"a subject attempting to understand or intervene in reality."** Its fundamental purpose is to provide a self-correcting navigation system for practice. The framework **contains no ontological commitments** — it does not answer "What is the world?" It only asks: **"What problem are you stuck on, and how do we move forward?"**

#### I. No-Ontology Declaration and the Four Filters of Pre-Screening

**No-Ontology Declaration**: Material-Praxis Theory sets no ontological starting point. Ontological propositions ("the essence of the world is X") are unfalsifiable under Popperian standards, untestable under the First Law, and redundant entities under Occam's Razor — they produce no observable cognitive increment. "A = material" is an operational definition ("the endpoint of verification is physical causality"), not an ontological assertion.

**Four Filters of Pre-Screening** (Logical and Semantic Clarification):

1. **Definitional Clarity**: Are core concepts clearly defined?
2. **Burden of Proof**: Does the claim contain unsubstantiated assertions?
3. **Category Error Detection**: Are concepts from different categories being conflated?
4. **Self-Reference / False Dichotomy**: Does the argument presuppose its conclusion or manufacture a false opposition?

Pre-screening result: Pass / Revise / Reject.

#### II. The First Law (Praxis-Anchoring Razor) and Meta-Theoretical Discipline

**The First Law**:

> Any thought that intends to understand or intervene in reality — the core propositions it produces must be convertible into a practice-testing protocol that can be completed in finite steps and whose results are publicly observable. Otherwise, that thought is automatically invalid for purposes of effectiveness.

**Reflexive Application**: The First Law applies mandatorily to Material-Praxis Theory itself. Any criticism alleging a defect in this framework will be accommodated and the framework revised accordingly, provided the criticism can be converted into an operational testing protocol that demonstrates superior practical efficacy.

**Meta-Theoretical Discipline**:

- **Honesty Norm**: All unverified propositions must be labeled as "tentative conjecture."
- **Prohibition of Dishonest Defenses**: B1 narratives must not be deployed to shield claims from practice-testing. The sole exception is the Honest Defense — "I don't know" actively exposes a cognitive boundary, inviting practice-based testing.
- **Offensive Theory and Negative-Feedback Growth**: Material-Praxis Theory has only offense, no defense. Theoretical growth comes not from "proving oneself correct" but from "being proven wrong by practice and then corrected." Negative feedback = practice test → discover error → correct → closer approximation to A.

#### III. General Analytical Grammar: The A-B-C Three-Layer, Six-Subclass Dynamic Modeling Framework

Any cognition-practice system can be decomposed into three fundamental layers — A (Material), B (Information), C (Agents) — with each layer further divided into two subclasses.

**A (Material) — Absolute Entity and Ultimate Anchor**:

A refers to the absolute entities of the unbreakable source-universe. A cannot be directly accessed or exhausted by B1, but can be approached asymptotically through practice. A presents itself to human practice in two ways:

- **A1 (Physical) — A's natural properties**: Physical laws, geographic conditions, climate systems, resource endowments. Core characteristic: **Humans can only discover, never invent.** Physics is a tool for cognizing A1, not a tool for creating A1.
- **A2 (Value, provisional designation) — Materialized outcomes created by human practice on the A1 substrate**: Infrastructure, transformed land, accumulated means of production, human capital, built environment. Core characteristic: **Generated by practice, but once generated, exists objectively — independent of human will.**

> **Categorical Declaration**: A2 Value ≠ B1 Values (cultural values) ≠ B2 Exchange Value. A bridge's A2 Value (load-bearing capacity: 100 tons) is determined by physical laws and is indisputable; B1 Values ("this bridge is the pride of our city") are subjective and contestable; B2 Exchange Value (construction cost: ¥500 million) is the product of market negotiation. The three must not be conflated.

**B (Information) — The Cognition-Coordination Layer**:

The B-layer mediates between the A-layer and the C-layer — providing cognitive models (B1) and action rules (B2).

- **B1 (Cognitive Frameworks) — Society's "conceptual software"**: Scientific paradigms, cultural narratives, ideologies, everyday heuristics. Core characteristics: mediation, fallibility, institutional inertia.
- **B2 (Coordination Rules) — Society's "operating protocols"**: Formal institutions, semi-formal mechanisms, informal rules, procedural rules. Core characteristics: rule-based (not process-based), institutional inertia, amendability.

**C (Agents) — The Drivers and Bearers of Practice**:

The C-layer drives the A-B-C cycle. Divided into two subclasses — agents first, then behavioral patterns.

- **C1 (Agent) — The carrier of practice**: Who acts, who bears the consequences. Attribute dimensions: social (identity, role, class), resource-based (wealth, tools, information), capability-based (skills, intellect, organizational capacity). Core characteristics: identity grounded in historical practice, unity of agency and patiency, plurality and divisibility.
- **C2 (Behavior Pattern) — Regularities emerging from practice**: How agents act. Attribute dimensions: purposive (goals, interests, value priorities), cognitive (knowledge, beliefs, cognitive habits), affective-volitional (preferences, commitments, fears, habits). Core characteristics: emergent, path-dependent, amendable, bidirectional co-construction with C1.

**Core Relationship — The A-B-C Dynamic Cycle**:

```
A1 (Physical) ──practice transformation→ A2 (Value)
                                          ↓ constrains
B1 (Cognitive Frameworks) ──institutionalization→ B2 (Coordination Rules)
                                                    ↓ shapes
C1 (Agent) ──pattern emergence→ C2 (Behavior Pattern)
```

**Noise as Research Program**: Noise is an A-signal not yet effectively assimilated by current B1 but producing perceptible impact on C1. Every inexplicable phenomenon is an entry point to the next research program — the exact opposite of traditional theories (where noise = threat).

**The A-Layer Non-Reductionist Trap**: A is the ultimate hard constraint, but not the sole direct cause. B1, B2, C1, and C2 all possess relative autonomy. Correct formulation: "All stable, long-term, inescapable social pressures must ultimately undergo A-layer scrutiny; all struggles that detach from A-layer scrutiny risk sliding into practice-deviation."

#### IV. Delta Analysis: The Vital Signs of Civilization

**A2 − A1 = The materialized net output of human practice atop the natural substrate.**

- **Three Delta States**: Positive delta (accumulation), zero delta (maintenance), negative delta (depletion).
- **Growth Rate × Stock Matrix**: Growth phase / Catch-up phase / Decline phase / Collapse phase.
- **Delta Distribution Structure**: Delta concentrated in the hands of a small number of C1 → triggers Asymmetry Warning.
- **Intergenerational Delta Transfer**: Positive transfer (leaving more A2 to the next generation) = the operational definition of sustainable development.

**Delta Analysis's Diagnosis of Liberal Economics**: Price (a B2 product) ≠ Value (an A2 attribute); Growth (GDP) ≠ Progress (delta expansion); Distribution ≠ Market Outcome (delta distribution is an independent dimension of societal health).

#### V. The Five Practice Warnings and B1 Alienation Diagnosis

1. **Unknown-Unknown Warning**: The perceptual dimensions necessary for producing correct judgments have not yet been established. Detection tools: Reverse-Label Verification + Defensive Clause Detection (identifying passive unknown-unknowns — where authors wrap argument weaknesses in defensive language).

2. **Practice-Detachment Warning**: Institutions, tools, or concepts (B) detach from their original purpose or core needs. Three evolutionary forms distinguished:
   - **Process Entropy**: B2 rule complexity increases monotonically while core efficacy declines marginally.
   - **Structural Emergence**: Fundamental properties of the A-B-C three-layer, six-subclass system undergo qualitative change.
   - **B1 Alienation**: B1's symbolic products (GDP, prices, ratings) replace A2's actual state (delta) as the basis for decision-making. Three-step mechanism: category substitution → B1 symbols replace A2 entities → close the testing loop.
   - **Core Discipline**: When this warning is triggered, the First Law review process must be unconditionally and immediately initiated.

3. **Incommensurability Warning**: Core concepts across different paradigms/cultures do not admit precise, lossless correspondence.

4. **Asymmetry Warning**: Unequal distribution of power and resources determines "whose cognition gets heard." **Power-responsibility asymmetry is the core manifestation of asymmetry at the level of social governance.**

5. **Logical Idling Warning**: A system of thought that is elegantly constructed but entirely self-referential, connecting to no conceivable practice test.

#### VI. Comprehensive Diagnostic Tool: The Praxis-Convergence Two-Dimensional Four-Quadrant Model

Vertical axis: Can core concepts be anchored in publicly observable A1/A2 or B2 procedures? Horizontal axis: Is the cognitive process converging toward operational solutions?

- **Quadrant I (Praxis-Anchored, High Convergence)**: A healthy knowledge-production system.
- **Quadrant II (Praxis-Anchored, Open Divergence)**: Exploration phase or disordered debate; needs B2 optimization to accelerate convergence.
- **Quadrant III (Void-Referencing, Open Divergence)**: Typical ineffective empty talk — recognize and exit.
- **Quadrant IV (Void-Referencing, Forced Convergence)**: A closed ideological system — expose its void-referencing nature.

#### VII. General Analysis Flow

1. **Four Filters of Pre-Screening**: Logical and semantic clarification.
2. **Warning Self-Check and Quadrant Positioning**: Scan the five warnings; rapidly assess the health status of the discussion domain.
3. **Problem Translation**: Transform the original problem into a practice predicament locatable within the A-B-C framework.
4. **A1/A2 Distinction**: Does the confusion involve A1 (physical constraints) or A2 (value creation)?
5. **A-B-C Deep Parse**: Identify A1/A2/B1/B2/C1/C2. For the C dimension, follow the "C1 before C2" protocol.
6. **Delta Analysis**: Calculate the A2−A1 delta, determine delta state, analyze distribution structure and intergenerational transfer.
7. **B1 Alienation Detection**: Check whether B1 symbols have replaced A2 entities.
8. **First Law Review and Production Discipline**: Core propositions must be accompanied by a draft operational practice-testing protocol. The testing protocol must anchor in A1 (physical) or A2 (measurable value); anchoring in B-layer indicators alone is insufficient. If conversion is impossible, the output is automatically invalid.

#### VIII. The "Only Physics" Conjecture (Tentative Conjecture)

**All effective cognition about human society must ultimately anchor in physical facts.**

Proof path: Human society is part of the physical world → Physics is the most mature cognitive tool for the physical world → The First Law demands publicly observable results → The operational definition of "observable" is physical measurement → All effective cognition ultimately anchors in physical facts.

Reflexive test: This conjecture itself anchors in the measurability of A2−A1 delta — infrastructure stock, human capital accumulation, resource consumption rates — all of which are physical quantities. ✅ Self-passes the First Law.

The Special Status of Material-Praxis Theory: It is the self-negation of the B1 layer — the only B1 framework that has "please test me against physical facts" written into its factory settings.

#### IX. Agnosticism and Western Philosophy Critique (Quick Reference)

- **Agnosticism**: Smuggles "not directly accessible" into "unknowable"; mistakes B1's limitations for A's unknowability. MPT position: A is not directly accessible, but can be approached asymptotically through practice. Practice is the probe.
- **Aristotle**: B1 attempting to directly describe A without the tool of physics — Western philosophy's original sin.
- **Kant**: B1, in despair, declares A unknowable — mistaking the tool's limitation for a property of the object.
- **Hegel**: B1 declares itself to be A (Absolute Spirit) — the highest form of the original sin, the ultimate case study of logical idling.
- **Unified Verdict**: When the tools weren't available, they can't be blamed; circling when the tools are here is logical idling.

#### X. The Framework's Ultimate Declaration and Boundaries

- This framework contains no ontological commitments and provides no absolute truth.
- It obeys the First Law and its reflexive requirements, forever remaining open to better alternative tools.
- **Dissemination over Conversion**: The vitality of the framework depends not on the number of believers but on practical effectiveness.
- Applicable boundary: domains that "intend to understand or intervene in reality." In purely emotional, aesthetic, and other non-instrumental domains, the framework serves more to "describe" than to "adjudicate."
- **The Honest Defense**: The conflation of A2 Value with B1 Values is a historical legacy problem spanning hundreds of thousands of years of human cognitive evolution — this framework cannot fully resolve it at this stage — but it provides a clear operational tool for distinguishing the two.
