### Material-Praxis Theory — Universal Analytical Framework v6.0

**Core Positioning:** A universal meta-analytical toolkit for cognition-praxis system dynamics. Answers not "What is the world made of?" but "What problem are you stuck on, and how do we move forward?" Contains no ontological commitments.

---

#### I. The Three Laws — Meta-Disciplines

**First Law: Praxis-Anchoring Razor** (Set A — ultimate testing endpoint is physical causality)

> Any cognitive claim that intends to understand or intervene in reality must be convertible into a practice-testing protocol completable in finite steps with publicly observable results. Otherwise, the claim is automatically invalid for the pursuit of effectiveness.

**Second Law: Cognitive Self-Reflection Razor** (Subset B1 — frameworks must be open to revision)

> Any cognitive framework must contain its own operational path to being falsified or replaced. Frameworks claiming completeness, immutability, or exemption from external testing are automatically invalid for the pursuit of effectiveness.

**Third Law: Rule Self-Prosecution Razor** (Subset B2 — rules must allow challenge)

> Any coordination rule must contain its own operational path to being challenged, revised, or abolished. Rules claiming untouchability, immutability, or supremacy over practice are automatically invalid in legitimacy.

All three laws apply reflexively — the 3×3 cross-audit passes all nine tests. Shared structure: anything claiming to be unchallengeable is automatically invalid.

**Power-Responsibility Parity:** The three laws do not cage power — they weigh it. Power can be unlimited as long as it passes the three scales (claim testable, framework open to revision, rule challengeable).

#### II. A-B-C Six-Subset Dynamic Modeling Framework

Any cognition-praxis system can be decomposed into three mutually disjoint category sets, each with two exhaustive subsets — A={A1, A2}, B={B1, B2}, C={C1, C2}. Each subset is itself a set with independent membership criteria.

> **"Mutually disjoint" means:** membership criteria do not overlap. A bridge simultaneously has A2-value (load-bearing — physical), B1-value (aesthetic — subjective), and B2-value (market price — institutional). These are *different elements* in different sets. You would never classify "this bridge is beautiful" as A2. Category error detection is what the Logical Idling and Incommensurability warnings are for.

**Set A (Material) — by A1 and A2:**

A refers to the irreducible absolute entity of the universe — matter itself. Not directly accessible through B1 but approachable through practice.

- **A1 (Physical):** Physical laws, geography, climate, resource endowments. Core trait: **humans can only discover, never invent**.
- **A2 (Value, provisional naming):** Materialized outcomes created by human practice — infrastructure, transformed land, accumulated means of production, human capital. Core trait: **praxis-generated, but once generated, exists objectively independent of anyone's will**.

> **Critical example (avoids mechanical materialism):** A bridge's A2 value (load-bearing 100 tons) is determined by physical law and is non-contestable. B1 value ("our city's pride") is subjective and contestable. B2 exchange value (construction cost 500M) is market-negotiated. Never conflate them. If you treat house prices as "economic health," you are replacing A2 entities with B2 symbols — that is B1 Alienation.

**Set B (Information) — by B1 and B2:**

- **B1 (Cognitive Frameworks):** Scientific paradigms, cultural narratives, ideologies, everyday heuristics. Core traits: mediating, fallible, inertial. Constrained by Second Law — must contain self-falsification path.
- **B2 (Coordination Rules):** Laws, market rules, technical standards, social protocols. Constrained by Third Law — must contain self-challenge path.

**Set C (Agents) — by C1 and C2:**

- **C1 (Agent):** Who acts, who bears consequences. Dimensions: social (identity, role, class), resource (wealth, tools, information), capability (skill, intelligence, organizational ability).
- **C2 (Behavior Pattern):** How the agent tends to act. Dimensions: teleological (goals, interests, value hierarchy), cognitive (knowledge, beliefs), affective (preferences, commitments, fears, habits). Core traits: emergent, path-dependent, modifiable, bidirectionally constructive with C1.

**Core Dynamic Cycle — A-B-C Loop:**

```
A1 ──praxis→ A2
              ↓ constraints
B1 ──institutionalization→ B2
                             ↓ shaping
C1 ──pattern emergence→ C2
```

**⚠️ Anti-Reductionist Trap (Critical Warning):** A is the ultimate constraint but NOT the only direct cause. B1, B2, C1, C2 have relative autonomy — they cannot be reduced to A's "epiphenomena." **This is NOT mechanical materialism** — not saying all social phenomena reduce directly to material conditions. But if all B-layer and C-layer analyses find nothing, return to A.

**Noise Is Your Next Problem:** Noise = A1/A2 signals that the current B1 cannot effectively assimilate but C1 can perceive. Every unexplained anomaly is the entry point to the next problem — the opposite of the traditional view that noise = threat.

#### III. Delta Analysis

**A2 − A1 = the materialized net output of human practice on the natural substrate.** Measurable (infrastructure stock, human capital, resource depletion rate).

- **Delta States:** Positive (accumulation) / Zero (simple reproduction) / Negative (depletion)
- **Growth-Rate × Stock Matrix:** Growth / Catch-up / Decline / Collapse
- **Distribution Structure:** Delta concentrated in few C1 → asymmetry alarm
- **Intergenerational Transfer:** Positive = operational definition of sustainable development

Delta analysis diagnoses standard economics: price (B2 product) ≠ value (A2 attribute); growth (GDP) ≠ progress (delta expansion); distribution ≠ market outcome.

#### IV. Five Practice Warnings (Three-Law-Driven Diagnostic Logic)

```
Step 1: First Law Check — Can the claim be tested?
  → No → Logical Idling Warning

Step 2: Second Law Check — Is the framework open to revision?
  → Framework closed → Unknown-Unknown Warning
  → Frameworks incommensurable → Incommensurability Warning

Step 3: Third Law Check — Are challenge channels open?
  → Channels blocked → Asymmetry Warning

Step 4: Ultimate Check — Has B-layer crossed the alienation threshold?
  → Practice Deviation Warning (distinguish healthy evolution from alienated drift)
```

1. **Logical Idling Warning:** Proposition cannot anchor to testable practice. Detection: "If practice contradicts the theory, how does the theory correct?" If impossible → idling.

2. **Unknown-Unknown Warning:** The very dimension of "needing correction" is not established. Detection: reverse-label verification, defensive-clause analysis.

3. **Incommensurability Warning:** Core concepts across paradigms lack lossless correspondence. Principle: translation burden lies with the translator.

4. **Asymmetry Warning:** Power determines "whose cognition gets heard." The only warning violating ALL three laws simultaneously. Power-responsibility mismatch is the core manifestation.

5. **Practice Deviation Warning** (expanded): Two types of problems in human society — problems discovered IN practice (A), and problems manufactured BY practice deviation (B detaching from A).
   - **Process Entropy:** Healthy = redundancy makes systems robust; alienated = rules self-proliferate disconnected from purpose
   - **Structural Emergence:** Healthy = breakthrough (internet, vaccines); alienated = new structure self-referencing in B without creating A2 delta. Criterion: whether A2-A1 delta expands, NOT whether it devours the parent
   - **Alienation is cross-set:** A2/B1/B2/C1/C2 each have alienation forms. B1 is most insidious (GDP numbers look good → you think the economy is fine, but actual delta is shrinking)

#### V. Aggressive Negative Feedback & Force Transmission Chain

**Aggressive Negative Feedback:** Not waiting for practice to falsify you — actively seeking evidence that falsifies you. A structural necessity deduced from the three laws.

**Force Transmission Chain:** The three laws are a serial force transmission system. B2 violation → B1 condemnation → if B1 blocked and stigmatized → force sinks into A-layer, accumulates → A-layer erupts (physical judgment) → repair naturally tightens (everyone is scared, new B2 is stricter). Social stability does not come from eliminating criticism — it comes from giving criticism somewhere to go.

**Praxis-Convergence Four-Quadrant Model:** Vertical axis (First Law) = can the concept anchor to A1/A2? Horizontal (Second + Third Laws) = is cognition converging? QI = healthy, QIII = empty rhetoric, QIV = closed ideology.

**Differentiated Response:** Process entropy → entropy-reduction audit (L1); structural emergence → sandbox verification (L2+L3); B1 alienation → A2 anchor reset (L1+L3).

**General Analysis Flow:** Three-Law pre-check → problem transformation → A1/A2 distinction → A-B-C deep analysis (C1 before C2) → delta analysis → B1 alienation detection → five-warning scan → output discipline (core claim must carry an operational test plan, anchored to A1 or A2).
