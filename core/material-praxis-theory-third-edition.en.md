## Abstract

This treatise systematically constructs **Material-Praxis Theory (MPT)** — a meta-theoretical framework anchored in the core postulate of "the primacy of practice," designed to provide universal methodological guidance for any cognitive activity that intends to understand or intervene in reality. Unlike traditional philosophy, which pursues static contemplation of truth, MPT repositions the core purpose of cognition as *effective intervention in practice*, and derives from this the principle that the fundamental task of cognition is *error identification* rather than *proof of correctness*.

From this foundation, MPT forges its supreme methodological principle — **the First Law (Praxis-Anchoring Razor)**: the validity of any cognitive claim depends on whether it can be converted into a practice-testing protocol completable in finite steps with publicly observable results.

## Core Architecture

To operationalize this principle, MPT constructs a **three-layer, six-subclass dynamic modeling grammar**:

- **A-Layer (Material Substrate)**: A1 (Physical — natural properties humans can only discover, never invent) and A2 (Value — materialized outcomes created by human practice that, once generated, exist objectively)
- **B-Layer (Information & Coordination)**: B1 (Cognitive Frameworks — scientific paradigms, cultural narratives, ideologies) and B2 (Coordination Rules — formal institutions, informal rules, procedural protocols)
- **C-Layer (Agents)**: C1 (Agent — who acts, who bears consequences) and C2 (Behavior Pattern — regularities emerging from practice)

This is not a static taxonomy but a **dynamic cycle**: A1 transforms into A2 through practice; A2 constrains B1, which institutionalizes into B2; B2 shapes C1, whose patterns emerge as C2 — feeding back into the entire system.

## Key Analytical Instruments

**Delta Analysis (A2 − A1)**: The materialized net output of human practice atop the natural substrate. This single metric yields an operational definition of sustainable development, a diagnostic for civilizational health (positive/zero/negative delta × growth rate matrix), and a framework for analyzing distributional justice and intergenerational equity.

**Five Practice Warnings**: An immunological toolkit for diagnosing common pathologies in cognition-practice systems:
1. **Unknown-Unknown Warning** — perceptual dimensions not yet established
2. **Practice-Detachment Warning** — institutions/tools/concepts detached from core purpose, including B1 Alienation (when symbolic products like GDP replace A2 actual states)
3. **Incommensurability Warning** — core concepts across paradigms lack precise correspondence
4. **Asymmetry Warning** — unequal power distribution determines "whose cognition gets heard"
5. **Logical Idling Warning** — elegantly self-referential thought systems disconnected from any conceivable practice test

**Praxis-Convergence Four-Quadrant Model**: A rapid diagnostic that plots any cognitive domain along two axes — praxis-anchoring (vertical) and convergence tendency (horizontal) — producing four possible states from healthy knowledge production (Quadrant I) to closed ideological systems (Quadrant IV).

## Intellectual Genealogy

MPT engages in deep dialogue with Marxist philosophy of practice (Marx's *Theses on Feuerbach*, Mao's *On Practice*), systems theory (Bertalanffy, Ashby), cybernetics (Wiener), and complexity science (Prigogine, Holland). It also conducts a critical confrontation with post-modernism: MPT affirms post-modernism's deconstruction of power and false narratives but resolutely rejects its slide into nihilism, establishing "praxis-anchoring" and "the irreducibility of the material" as the foundation for rebuilding human cognition.

## Theoretical Innovations

- **No-Ontology Declaration**: MPT contains no ontological commitments. "A = material" is an operational definition, not an ontological assertion. The framework answers "What problem are you stuck on, and how do we move forward?" — not "What is the world made of?"
- **Reflexive First Law**: The First Law applies to MPT itself. Any criticism convertible into an operational test demonstrating superior efficacy will cause the framework to self-correct.
- **B1 Alienation as a Diagnostic Category**: The treatise provides the first systematic operationalization of the phenomenon where cognitive-symbolic products (prices, ratings, GDP) replace material reality as the object of decision-making — with a three-step mechanism for detection.
- **"Only Physics" Conjecture**: All effective cognition about human society must ultimately anchor in physical facts — a tentative conjecture that itself passes the First Law's reflexive test.

## Ultimate Commitment

Material-Praxis Theory does not promise eternal truth. It provides a cognitive operating manual for a complex, uncertain world — one capable of continuous self-correction. Its highest commitment is to equip its users with the capacity to question, improve, and ultimately transcend the framework itself.
